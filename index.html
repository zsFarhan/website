FAQ

Do we need perfect data to start?

No. We start by instrumenting the right signals and defining metrics. Many teams unlock value quickly with targeted data improvements rather than "boil the ocean" data projects.

Do you build, or only advise?

Both. We provide strategy and work hands-on with your team through design and build support, while your team owns implementation.

How do you decide between LLMs, classical ML, or hybrid approaches?

We choose based on measurable requirements: latency, cost, accuracy, safety, interpretability, and the data you already have (or can realistically collect).

How long until we see results?

The Readiness & Validation Sprint produces clarity and a plan in six weeks. A deeper engagement over three months can produce a production-grade pilot design and support your team through build phases.

Will our team be able to maintain what we build?

We prioritize maintainable design, documentation, and knowledge transfer. The goal is to build the process and internal capability that enables your team to maintain and extend the system without dependency.
